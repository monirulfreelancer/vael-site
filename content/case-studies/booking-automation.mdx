## The challenge

The team was managing bookings across phone, email, and three separate
spreadsheets. Double-bookings were frequent, and one staff member spent
nearly half her week just confirming appointments manually.

## The solution

We built a custom booking platform with real-time availability, automated
SMS and email confirmations, and a unified admin dashboard. Staff see every
location in one view; customers self-book in under a minute. Stack: Next.js,
PostgreSQL, Twilio, deployed on a self-managed VPS.

![Admin dashboard](/work/booking-shot.svg)

## Results

- Eliminated double-bookings entirely with live availability locking
- Cut admin time by 20+ hours per week across the team
- Customers now book 3x faster with the self-serve flow
- Owner reclaimed evenings previously spent on manual confirmations
