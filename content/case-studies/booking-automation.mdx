## The challenge
The team ran bookings across phone, email, and three separate spreadsheets. Double bookings happened weekly, and one staff member spent half her week confirming appointments by hand.

## The solution
We built a booking platform with live availability, automatic SMS and email confirmations, and one dashboard covering every location. Customers book themselves in under a minute. Stack: Next.js, PostgreSQL, Twilio, deployed on a self-managed VPS.

![Admin dashboard](/work/booking-shot.svg)

## Results
- Eliminated double bookings with live availability locking
- Cut admin time by more than 20 hours per week across the team
- Customers book three times faster through the self-serve flow
- The owner reclaimed evenings previously spent confirming appointments
