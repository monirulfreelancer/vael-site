## The challenge
The legacy store was slow, hard to update, and losing mobile customers. Inventory changes took days, and the checkout leaked sales at every step.

## The solution
We rebuilt the storefront on a fast headless architecture with AI product recommendations and a streamlined one-page checkout. The team updates inventory and content themselves in minutes. Stack: Next.js, headless commerce, and an AI recommendation engine.

![Storefront](/work/ecom-shot.svg)

## Results
- Online revenue up 38 percent in the first quarter
- Page load dropped from 4.2 seconds to under one second
- Inventory updates now take minutes, saving over 12 hours weekly
- Mobile conversion improved after the checkout redesign
