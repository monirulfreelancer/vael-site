## The challenge
A growing SaaS team was buried in repetitive support tickets. Response times stretched into hours, and hiring more agents was not an option at their stage.

## The solution
We built a RAG-based support agent trained on their docs, past tickets, and product knowledge. It answers in their brand voice, escalates edge cases to humans, and improves from every resolved conversation. Stack: Next.js, a vector database, an LLM API, and custom escalation logic.

![Agent console](/work/agent-shot.svg)

## Results
- 68 percent of incoming tickets resolved without a human
- First response time cut from hours to under 30 seconds
- Support runs around the clock with no added headcount
- Human agents now focus on complex, high-value cases
