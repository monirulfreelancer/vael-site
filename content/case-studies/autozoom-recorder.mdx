## The challenge
Screen recordings look flat without manual zoom and pan editing, and the leading tool in this space only runs on Mac. Windows users had no real option.

## The solution
We built AutoZoom, an AI screen recorder that detects the moments worth zooming into and applies smooth camera movement automatically. Record, let it edit itself, export.

## Results
- Automatic zoom and pan with no manual editing
- Runs on Windows, where the main competitor does not
- Simple lifetime pricing at 69 dollars
- This is our own product, live at autozoom.app
