## The challenge
Cold email teams in our market needed bulk verification, but global tools do not support local payment rails, and most have no admin controls for reselling credits to a team.

## The solution
We built a full verification platform: single and bulk checks with an API, a three-tier role system, admin-managed credit packages, and a manual payment flow supporting bKash, Rocket, Nagad, and bank transfer, with an approve and reject queue for the admin.

## Results
- A working credit economy with admin approval flows
- X-API-Key authenticated endpoints for single and batch verification
- A blog and SEO layer that brings organic signups
- This is our own product, live at goanglelead.com
