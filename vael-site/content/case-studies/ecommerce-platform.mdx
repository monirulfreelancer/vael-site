## The challenge

The brand's legacy store was slow, hard to update, and bleeding mobile
customers. Inventory updates took days and the checkout flow leaked sales at
every step.

## The solution

We rebuilt the storefront from the ground up with a fast headless
architecture, AI-powered product recommendations, and a streamlined
one-page checkout. The team can now update inventory and content themselves
in minutes. Stack: Next.js, headless commerce, AI recommendation engine.

![Storefront](/work/ecom-shot.svg)

## Results

- 38% increase in online revenue within the first quarter
- Page load dropped from 4.2s to under 1s
- Inventory updates now take minutes, saving 12+ hours weekly
- Mobile conversion rate up significantly after checkout redesign
