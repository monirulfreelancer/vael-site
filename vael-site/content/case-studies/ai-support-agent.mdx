## The challenge

A growing SaaS team was drowning in repetitive support tickets. Response
times stretched to hours, and hiring more agents was not sustainable at their
stage.

## The solution

We built a RAG-based AI support agent trained on their docs, past tickets,
and product knowledge. It answers in their brand voice, escalates edge cases
to humans, and learns from every resolved conversation. Stack: Next.js,
vector database, LLM API, custom escalation logic.

![Agent console](/work/agent-shot.svg)

## Results

- 68% of incoming tickets resolved without human involvement
- Average first response cut from hours to under 30 seconds
- Support now runs 24/7 with no added headcount
- Human agents freed to handle high-value, complex cases
